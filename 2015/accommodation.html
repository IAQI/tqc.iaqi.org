Accommodation

The conference will be held in Brussels, at the Solbosch campus of the Université libre de Bruxelles in Ixelles.

A list of suggested hotels, apartments and Bed & Breakfast is given below. Other accomodation possibilities can be found on webistes such as TripAdvisor or Airbnb. All hotels are between 20 and 40 minutes from the conference venue by public transport.
